How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via WhatsApp and email. You can track your delivery in real-time via the courier's app.

Yes, 100%. Every product goes through our 12-point authentication check. We source directly from authorized partners only — no replicas, ever.
How long does delivery take?
Karachi: 1-2 business days. Lahore & Islamabad: 2-3 days. Other cities: 3-5 business days. Express options available.

Absolutely! WhatsApp us at 03491705092. Share which product you want, your size, and delivery address — we'll confirm and send payment details.

Yes! 30-day hassle-free returns. Items must be unworn with original tags. WhatsApp us at 03491705092 to initiate a return or exchange.
